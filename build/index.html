Welcome to SOLON GLOBAL!

SOLON GLOBAL stands as an example of excellence in the rapidly evolving world of investments and finance. As a boutique private investment firm, our commitment isn't just to financial growth. We are dedicated to creating a lasting positive impact. We passionately believe in the power of investments to transform shareholders' aspirations, empower businesses, and help communities.

Our Mission

We go beyond numbers

Our mission is not simply about numbers and the bottom line but about creating value. Our highly skilled team is well-equipped with a wide range of tools, encompassing private equity, funding, and venture capital techniques. We operate across a spectrum of industries and regions, with our expansive regional and international networks playing a crucial role in securing stable returns and adopting industry best practices. We strive to ensure that our financial success is closely intertwined with the social and environmental progress of the areas in which we operate.

Bespoke Solutions

Discover a world of unique opportunities

At SOLON GLOBAL, we understand that every investment is a unique challenge. That is why we specialize in crafting tailor-made solutions, carefully identifying and leveraging global opportunities. Our gaze is particularly sharp on the GCC and APAC regions. A glance at our portfolio reveals our diverse interests, ranging from Commercial and Industrial Enterprises to Distressed Assets, Energy, IT, and Startups. Whether it is an already operational company or a fledgling startup, we are there to nurture, forge long-lasting partnerships, and cultivate tomorrow's industry leaders. This individual approach allows us to be agile and responsive to the ever-changing market dynamics.

Industry Expertise

Decisions based on unique experience

Our track record is a testament to our profound industry knowledge and tenacity. More than just investors, we often step into the role of strategic partners. We are open to board memberships, offering a treasure trove of business acumen and industry insights. This depth of engagement gives us a comprehensive understanding of the businesses we invest in and drives our ability to add tangible value.

Strategic Approach

Where investment precision meets passion

We pride ourselves on our meticulous investment approach, rooted in comprehensive risk assessment and exhaustive due diligence. Our expertise lies in driving operational enhancements, rooting out causes of inefficiencies, and steering our portfolio companies towards their best results for all stakeholders. Whether it's unique funding avenues, co-investing opportunities, or providing liquidity, we are always a step ahead. Our strategic approach also includes a robust exit strategy framework, designed to reach the desired gains and facilitate seamless transitions.

A Commitment to Innovation

We use the latest tools to get the best results

In the dynamic world of finance, standing still is akin to moving backward. SOLON GLOBAL relentlessly pursues innovation, consistently incorporating cutting-edge analytical and statistical models. All this ensures that we remain flexible, ready to adapt, and ultimately successful. Our commitment to innovation extends beyond investments. It encompasses our entire corporate ethos, promoting a culture of continuous improvement and learning.

Our Values

Integrity and accountability

At SOLON GLOBAL, our corporate values are anchored in unwavering integrity and steadfast accountability. We conduct our business with the highest degree of transparency and ethical standards, ensuring trust and reliability in all our dealings. Our investment approach is grounded in adhering to ethical investment practices. We commit to responsible stewardship of our investments, aiming for financial robustness while contributing positively to the broader community.

Cookies Policy

A cookie is a small piece of data, which includes an identifier made of letters and numbers that is stored on your computer (device) and/or in your browser.

We may use the following types of cookies:
- Strictly necessary cookies
These are essential for you to browse our website and use its core/main features.
- Functional and Performance cookies
These help us understand how you use our site so we can improve its functionality.
- Targeting (Advertising, Marketing cookies)

These are typically provided by third parties to help deliver relevant content for you. Information about website usage and visits may be shared with other organisations such as advertisers.

How to block all cookies

If you want to block all cookies, you may be able to do this through the settings in your browser for each corresponding browser you use and each corresponding device you use. If you block all cookies you may find that certain features on our website will not function properly and we may not be able to respond to your request or provide you the best service.
