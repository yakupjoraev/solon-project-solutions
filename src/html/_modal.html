Cookies Policy

A cookie is a small piece of data, which includes an identifier made of letters and numbers that is stored on your computer (device) and/or in your browser.

We may use the following types of cookies:
- Strictly necessary cookies
These are essential for you to browse our website and use its core/main features.
- Functional and Performance cookies
These help us understand how you use our site so we can improve its functionality.
- Targeting (Advertising, Marketing cookies)

These are typically provided by third parties to help deliver relevant content for you. Information about website usage and visits may be shared with other organisations such as advertisers.

How to block all cookies

If you want to block all cookies, you may be able to do this through the settings in your browser for each corresponding browser you use and each corresponding device you use. If you block all cookies you may find that certain features on our website will not function properly and we may not be able to respond to your request or provide you the best service.
